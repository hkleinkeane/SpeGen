/SpeGen is an Android AAC app that is in active development and will be free upon release. The goal of this project is to be a budget-friendly and accessible app that anyone can use for communication. If you want to support development as an AAC user or a caretaker of someone who uses an AAC device or app without directly contributing, check out this form: https://forms.gle/TBRE1EQvizbBx7Kk9.
